About

Subseasonal variability, BSISO & the Indian Summer Monsoon

I’m a climate scientist focusing on the dynamics and predictability of subseasonal variability (2–8 weeks), especially the Boreal Summer Intraseasonal Oscillation (BSISO) and the Madden–Julian Oscillation (MJO). My work blends reanalysis (ERA5), aerosol–cloud datasets (CAMS), and numerical weather prediction (UK Met Office UM) to understand convection, rainfall, and multi-scale interactions over India and the tropical Indo-Pacific.

What is BSISO?

The dominant summer intraseasonal mode over the Asian monsoon, with northward & eastward propagation shaping active/break spells and extremes.

What is the MJO?
