I am a climate scientist pursuing my Ph.D. at the National Centre for Medium Range Weather Forecasting (NCMRWF), Noida, India. My research investigates subseasonal variability with a particular emphasis on:

  • Indian Summer Monsoon dynamics
  • Madden–Julian Oscillation (MJO) & Boreal Summer Intraseasonal Oscillation (BSISO)
  • Convective instability metrics (CAPE, CIN)
  • Monsoon prediction and Unified Model diagnostics
